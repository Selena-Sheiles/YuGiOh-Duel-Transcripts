World Legacy Guardragon
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only activate 1 "World Legacy Guardragon" per turn.

①: When this card is activated: You can target 1 Dragon monster in your GY with a Level of 4 or lower; add it to your hand or Special Summon it.

②: Once per turn: You can target 1 Dragon monster you control; move that monster you control to another of your Main Monster Zones.
