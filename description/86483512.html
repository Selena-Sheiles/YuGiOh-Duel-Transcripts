You can only use effect ① of "Virtual World Roshi - Laolao" once per turn.

①: If this card is in your hand: You can target 1 "Virtual World" card you control; send 1 "Virtual World" card of a different type from your Deck to the GY, and if you do, Special Summon this card, then, you can Special Summon a "Virtual World" monster from your GY in Defense Position, with a different name from the card sent to the GY, but negate its effects. For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon monsters with a Level/Rank of 3 or higher.
