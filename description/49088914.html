[Psychic / Tuner / Effect]

You can only use effect ① of "Virtual World Mai-Hime - Lulu" once per turn.

①: If this card is in your hand: You can target 1 "Virtual World" card you control; send 1 "Virtual World" card of a different type from your Deck to the GY, and if you do, Special Summon this card, then, you can add 1 "Virtual World" card of the third type from your Deck to your hand, except "Virtual World Mai-Hime - Lulu". For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon monsters with a Level/Rank of 3 or higher.
