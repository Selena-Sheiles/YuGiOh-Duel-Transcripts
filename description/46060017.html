Zoodiac Barrage
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ① of "Zoodiac Barrage" once per turn.

①: You can target 1 face-up card you control; destroy it, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Zoodiac" monster from your Deck.

②: If this card is destroyed by a card effect and sent to the GY: You can target 1 "Zoodiac" Xyz Monster you control; attach this card to that Xyz Monster as material.
