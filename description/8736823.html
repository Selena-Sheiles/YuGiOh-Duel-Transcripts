Virtual World Hime - Nyannyan
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Wind
Level 3
Atk/1500 Def/1500
[Psychic / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Virtual World Hime - Nyannyan" once per turn.

①: If a Level 3 monster(s) is Normal or Special Summoned to your field: You can Special Summon this card from your GY as a Tuner, but banish it when it leaves the field. For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon Level/Rank 3 or higher monsters.

②: If this card is banished: You can target 1 of your other banished card; shuffle it into the Deck.
