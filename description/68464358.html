Level 4

[Dragon / Tuner / Effect]

You can only use effect ① of "Rokket Tracer" once per turn.

①: (Quick Effect): You can target 1 face-up card you control; destroy it, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Rokket" monster from your Deck, except "Rokket Tracer". For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon DARK monsters from the Extra Deck.
