Red-Eyes Fusion
Card type Spell
Property Normal

You can only activate 1 "Red-Eyes Fusion" per turn. You cannot Normal or Special Summon other monsters the turn you activate this card.

①: Fusion Summon 1 Fusion Monster that lists a "Red-Eyes" monster as material, using monsters from your hand, Deck, or field as Fusion Materials. That monster's name becomes "Red-Eyes Black Dragon".
