Phantom Knights' Fog Blade
Card type Trap
Property Continuous

Activate this card by targeting 1 Effect Monster on the field. You can only use effect ② of "Phantom Knights' Fog Blade" once per turn.

①: The targeted monster has its effects negated, also it cannot attack or be targeted for attacks. When it leaves the field, destroy this card.

②: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights" monster in your GY: Special Summon it, but banish it when it leaves the field.
