Madolche Chateau
Card type Spell
Property Field

①: When this card is activated: Shuffle all "Madolche" monsters in your GY into the Deck.

②: All "Madolche" monsters on the field gain 500 ATK/DEF.

③: If a monster in your GY would be returned to the Deck by the effect of a "Madolche" monster, you can add it to your hand instead.
