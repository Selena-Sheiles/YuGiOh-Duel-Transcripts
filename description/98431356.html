Phantom Knights' Wing
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only use effect ② of "Phantom Knights' Wing" once per turn.

①: Target 1 face-up monster on the field; it gains 500 ATK, also this turn, the next time that target would be destroyed by battle or card effect, it is not destroyed.

②: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights" monster in your GY; Special Summon it, but banish it when it leaves the field.
