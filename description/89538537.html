Altergeist Silquitous
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Wind
Level 2
Atk/1200 Def/800
[Spellcaster / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Altergeist Silquitous" once per turn.

①: (Quick Effect): You can return 1 other "Altergeist" card you control to the hand, then target 1 card your opponent controls; return it to the hand.

②: If this card is sent from the field to the GY: You can target 1 "Altergeist" Trap in your GY; add it to your hand.
