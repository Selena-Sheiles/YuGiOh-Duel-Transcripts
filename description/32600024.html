Chamber Dragonmaid

Level 4

[Dragon / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Chamber Dragonmaid" once per turn.

①: If this card is Normal or Special Summoned: You can add 1 "Dragonmaid" Spell/Trap from your Deck to your hand.

②: At the start of the Battle Phase: You can return this card to your hand, and if you do, Special Summon 1 Level 7 or higher "Dragonmaid" monster from your hand or GY.
