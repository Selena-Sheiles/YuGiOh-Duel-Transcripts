[Wyrm / Effect]

You can only use effect ② of "Virtual World Oto-Hime - Toutou" once per turn.

①: If you control no monsters except Psychic and Wyrm monsters, you can Normal Summon this card without Tributing.

②: You can discard 1 Psychic or Wyrm monster; Special Summon this card from your GY as a Tuner, but banish it when it leaves the field. For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon Level/Rank 3 or higher monsters.
