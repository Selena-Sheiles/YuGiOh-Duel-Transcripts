Rokket Recharger
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 4
Atk/0 Def/2100
[Dragon / Effect]

You can only use effect ① of "Rokket Recharger" once per turn.

①: If a DARK monster you control that was Special Summoned from the Extra Deck is destroyed by battle or card effect: You can send this card from your hand or field to the GY, then target the destroyed monster; Special Summon 1 DARK monster from your GY whose original name is different from that of the targeted monster.

②: While you control a DARK monster that was Special Summoned from the Extra Deck, your opponent cannot target this card for attacks.
