Madolche Anjelly
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Earth
Level 4

[Fairy / Effect]

You can only use effect ① of "Madolche Anjelly" once per turn.

①: You can Tribute this card; Special Summon 1 "Madolche" monster from your Deck, but shuffle it into the Deck during the End Phase of your next turn. That monster cannot be destroyed by battle.

②: If this card in your possession is destroyed by your opponent and sent to the GY: Shuffle this card into the Deck.
