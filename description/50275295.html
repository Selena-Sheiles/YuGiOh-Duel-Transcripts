Virtual World Gate - Qinglong
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Virtual World Gate - Qinglong" once per turn.

①: You can banish 1 "Virtual World" card from your GY, then target 1 face-up monster on the field; negate its effects until the end of this turn.

②: You can banish this card from your GY; add 1 "Virtual World" monster from your Deck to your hand, then send 1 card from your hand to the GY.
