Madolche Petingcessoeur
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Earth
Level 4
Atk/1400 Def/1400
[Fairy / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Madolche Petingcessoeur" once per turn.

①: If you have no monster in your GY: You can Special Summon this card from your hand.

②: If this card is Special Summoned: You can Special Summon 1 "Madolche" monster from your hand or Deck, except "Madolche Petingcessoeur", and if you do, reduce its Level by 1. For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon "Madolche" monsters.

③: If this card in your possession is destroyed by an opponent's card and sent to the GY: Shuffle this card into the Deck.
