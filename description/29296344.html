Quadborrel Dragon
Card type Link Monster
Attribute Dark
Link-2 Left, Bottom
Atk/1000
[Dragon / Link / Effect]
2 Dragon monsters, including a "Rokket" monster

You can only use effect ① of "Quadborrel Dragon" once per turn.

①: You can discard 1 card, then target 1 face-up monster on the field; destroy that target, then, if it was a Link Monster, you can Special Summon from your hand or GY any number of "Rokket" monsters, up to its Link Rating, with different names from each other. For the rest of this turn, you cannot Special Summon Link-2 or lower monsters from the Extra Deck.
