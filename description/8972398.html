Omni Dragon Brotaur
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 1
Atk/0 Def/0
[Dragon / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Omni Dragon Brotaur" once per turn.

①: If a monster you control is destroyed by battle or an opponent's card effect: You can Special Summon this card from your hand or GY, but banish it when it leaves the field.

②: If this card is Special Summoned: You can target 1 face-up monster on the field; discard 1 card, and if you do, add 1 monster from your Deck to your hand, with the same Type and Attribute as that target, but a different name.
