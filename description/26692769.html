[Warrior / Link / Effect]
2+ DARK monsters

You can only use effect ① and ② of "The Phantom Knights of Rusty Bardiche" once per turn. This card cannot be used as Link Material.

①: During your Main Phase: You can send 1 "The Phantom Knights" monster from your Deck to the GY, then Set 1 "Phantom Knights" Spell/Trap directly from your Deck.

②: If a DARK Xyz Monster is Special Summoned to a zone this card points to: You can target 1 card on the field; destroy it.
