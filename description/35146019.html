Altergeist Manifestation
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only use effect ② of "Altergeist Manifestation" once per turn.

①: Target 1 "Altergeist" monster in your GY; Special Summon it in Attack Position, and if you do, equip it with this card. When this card leaves the field, destroy that monster.

②: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Altergeist" Trap in your GY; add it to your hand.
