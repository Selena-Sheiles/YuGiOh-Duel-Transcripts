The Phantom Knights of Torn Scales
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 3
Atk/600 Def/1600
[Warrior / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "The Phantom Knights of Torn Scales" once per turn.

①: You can discard 1 card; send 1 "Phantom Knights" card from your Deck to the GY.

②: If another "Phantom Knights" card in your GY is banished: You can Special Summon this card from your GY, but banish it when it leaves the field.
