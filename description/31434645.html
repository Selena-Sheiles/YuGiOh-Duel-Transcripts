Cursed Eldland
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ② and ③ of "Cursed Eldland" once per turn.

①: You can only declare attacks with Zombie monsters.

②: You can pay 800 LP; add 1 "Eldlich" monster or 1 "Golden Land" Spell/Trap from your Deck to your hand.

③: If this card is sent from the Spell & Trap Zone to your GY: You can send 1 "Eldlich" monster or 1 "Golden Land" Spell/Trap from your Deck to the GY.
