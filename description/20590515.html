Conquistador of the Golden Land
Card type Trap
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Conquistador of the Golden Land" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: Special Summon this card as a Normal Monster (Zombie/LIGHT/Level 5/ATK 500/DEF 1800) (this card is also still a Trap), then, if you control "Eldlich the Golden Lord", you can destroy 1 face-up card on the field.

②: During the End Phase: You can banish this card from your GY; Set 1 "Eldlixir" Spell/Trap directly from your Deck.
