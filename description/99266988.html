Chaos Space
Card type Spell
Property Normal

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Chaos Space" once per turn.

①: Send 1 LIGHT or DARK monster from your hand to the GY; add 1 monster with the other Attribute with a Level from 4 to 8 that cannot be Normal Summoned/Set from your Deck to your hand.

②: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 of your banished LIGHT or DARK monsters that cannot be Normal Summoned/Set; place that monster on the bottom of the Deck, then draw 1 card.
