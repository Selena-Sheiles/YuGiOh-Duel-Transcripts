Virtual World Gate - Xuanwu
Card type Trap
Property Continuous

You can only activate 1 "Virtual World Gate - Xuanwu" per turn. You can only use effect ① and ② of "Virtual World Gate - Xuanwu" once per turn.

①: During the Battle Phase, if you control another "Virtual World Gate" card: You can target 1 face-up monster on the field; change its battle position.

②: During your Main Phase: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Virtual World" monster in your GY; Special Summon it, but negate its effects, then send 1 card from your hand to the GY.
