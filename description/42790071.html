Altergeist Multifaker
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 3
Atk/1200 Def/800
[Spellcaster / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Altergeist Multifaker" once per turn.

①: If you activate a Trap Card: You can Special Summon this card from your hand.

②: You can only Special Summon "Altergeist" monsters during the turn you activate this effect. If this card is Special Summoned: You can Special Summon 1 "Altergeist" monster from your Deck in Defense Position, except "Altergeist Multifaker".
