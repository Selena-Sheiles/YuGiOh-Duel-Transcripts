Chaos Ruler, the Chaotic Magical Dragon
Card type Synchro Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 8
Atk/3000 Def/2500
[Dragon / Synchro / Effect]
1 Tuner + 1+ non-Tuner monsters

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Chaos Ruler, the Chaotic Magical Dragon" once per turn.

①: If this card is Synchro Summoned: You can excavate the top 5 cards of your Deck, and if you do, you can add 1 excavated LIGHT or DARK monster to your hand, also send the rest to the GY.

②: You can banish from your hand or GY 1 LIGHT and 1 DARK monster; Special Summon this card from your GY, but banish it when it leaves the field.
