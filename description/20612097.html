Eldlixir of Scarlet Sanguine
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Eldlixir of Scarlet Sanguine" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: Special Summon 1 Zombie monster from your Deck or GY, also for the rest of this turn, you cannot Special Summon monsters, except Zombie monsters. If you control no "Eldlich" monsters, this effect can only Special Summon "Eldlich" monsters.

②: You can banish this card from your GY; Set 1 "Golden Land" Spell/Trap directly from your Deck.
