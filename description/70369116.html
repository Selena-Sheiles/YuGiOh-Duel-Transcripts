Link-2 Bottom-Left, Bottom-Right

[Plant / Link / Effect]
2 Effect Monsters

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Predaplant Verte Anaconda" once per turn.

①: You can target 1 face-up monster on the field; it becomes DARK until the end of this turn.

②: You can pay 2000 LP and send 1 "Fusion" or "Polymerization" Normal or Quick-Play Spell from your Deck to the GY; this effect becomes that Spell's effect when it is activated. For the rest of this turn, you cannot Special Summon monsters.
