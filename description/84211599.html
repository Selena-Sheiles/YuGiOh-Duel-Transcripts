Pot of Prosperity
Card type Spell
Property Normal

You can only activate 1 "Pot of Prosperity" per turn. You cannot draw cards by card effects the turn you activate this card.

①: Banish face-down 3 or 6 cards of your choice from your Extra Deck; excavate cards from the top of your Deck equal to the number of cards banished by this effect, add 1 of them to your hand, and place the rest on the bottom of your Deck in any order. For the rest of this turn, any damage your opponent takes is halved.
