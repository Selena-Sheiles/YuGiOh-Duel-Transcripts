Madolche Hootcake
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Earth
Level 3
Atk/1500 Def/1100
[Beast / Effect]

You can only use effect ① of "Madolche Hootcake" once per turn.

①: You can target 1 monster in your GY; banish it, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Madolche" monster from your Deck, except "Madolche Hootcake".

②: If this card in your possession is destroyed by an opponent's card and sent to the GY: Shuffle this card into the Deck.
