Eldlixir of White Destiny
Card type Spell
Property Quick

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Eldlixir of White Destiny" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: Special Summon 1 Zombie monster from your hand or GY, but if you control no "Eldlich" monsters, this effect can only Special Summon "Eldlich" monsters. For the rest of this turn, you can only Special Summon Zombie monsters.

②: During your Main Phase: You can banish this card from your GY; Set 1 "Golden Land" Spell/Trap directly from your Deck.
