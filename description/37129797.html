Vampire Sucker
Card type Link Monster
Attribute Dark
Link-2 Bottom-Left, Bottom-Right
Atk/1600
[Zombie / Link / Effect]
2 Zombie monsters

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Vampire Sucker" once per turn.

①: You can target 1 monster in your opponent's GY; Special Summon it to your opponent's field in Defense Position as a Zombie.

②: If a monster(s) is Special Summoned from either GY: Draw 1 card.

③: If you would Tribute monsters you control for a Tribute Summon, you can Tribute Zombie monsters your opponent controls.
