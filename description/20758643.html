Graff, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss
Card type Effect Monster
Attribute Dark
Level 3
Atk/1000 Def/1500
[Fiend / Effect]

You can only use effect ① and ③ of "Graff, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: If you control no Spells/Traps: You can Special Summon this card from your hand.

②: If you control a monster that is not a "Burning Abyss" monster, destroy this card.

③: If this card is sent to the GY: You can Special Summon 1 "Burning Abyss" monster from your Deck, except "Graff, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss".
