Madolche Ticket
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ① of "Madolche Ticket" once per turn.

①: If a "Madolche" card from your field or GY is returned to your hand or Main Deck by a card effect: Add 1 "Madolche" monster from your Deck to your hand, but if you control a Fairy "Madolche" monster, you can Special Summon it in face-up Attack Position instead.
