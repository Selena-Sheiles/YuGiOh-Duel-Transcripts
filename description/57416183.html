Dragonmaid Tidying
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Dragonmaid Tidying" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: Target 1 Dragon monster you control and 1 card your opponent controls or in their GY; return them to the hand.

②: You can banish this card from your GY; Special Summon 1 "Dragonmaid" monster from your hand or GY in Defense Position, but return it to the hand during the End Phase.
