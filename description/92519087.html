Virtual World Kyubi - Shenshen
Card type Synchro Monster
Attribute Wind
Level 9
Atk/2800 Def/2400
[Psychic / Synchro / Effect]
1 Tuner + 1+ non-Tuner monsters

You can only use effect ② and ③ of "Virtual World Kyubi - Shenshen" once per turn.

①: Any card sent from the field to the GY is banished instead.

②: When your monster declares an attack: You can return 1 banished monster to the owner's GY.

③: During your Main Phase, except the turn this card was sent to the GY: You can banish 2 other monsters from your GY with different original Types and Attributes from each other; Special Summon this card from your GY.
