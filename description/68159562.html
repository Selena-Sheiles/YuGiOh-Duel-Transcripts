Madolche Promenade
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only use effect ① and ② of "Madolche Promenade" once per turn, and only one of them each turn.

①: Target 1 face-up card your opponent controls and 1 "Madolche" monster from your field or GY; negate the effects of that opponent's card until the end of this turn, and if you do, return your monster to the hand.

②: You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Madolche" Xyz Monster you control; attach to it as material 1 "Madolche" monster from your hand, Deck, or GY.
