Dogmatika Punishment
Card type Trap
Property Normal

You can only activate 1 "Dogmatika Punishment" per turn.

①: Target 1 face-up monster your opponent controls; send 1 monster with an equal or higher ATK from your Extra Deck to the GY, and if you do, destroy that monster. Until the end of your next turn, you cannot Special Summon monsters from the Extra Deck.
