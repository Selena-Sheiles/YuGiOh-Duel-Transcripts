Madolche Salon
Card type Spell
Property Continuous

You can only use effect ② of "Madolche Salon" once per turn.

①: During your Main Phase, you can Normal Summon 1 "Madolche" monster in addition to your Normal Summon/Set. (You can only gain this effect once per turn.)

②: If a "Madolche" card from your field or GY is returned to your hand or Main Deck by a card effect: Set 1 "Madolche" Spell/Trap from your Deck.
